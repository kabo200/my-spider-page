Jumping Spider

Jumping spiders are small, agile hunters known for their excellent vision and ability to jump long distances to catch prey.

Tarantula

Tarantulas are large, hairy spiders that are often kept as pets. They are generally docile but can deliver a painful bite if threatened.

wolf spider

wolf spiders are fast hunting spiders that do not build webs.they are usually brown,grey and black in color,they have eight eyes and very good eyesight

garden spider

they are common spiders found gardens and fields ,they are usually black,yellow,or orange in color .Garden spiders wait in the center of their web for prey
