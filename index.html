Popular Spider Species

Click a category to filter

Jumping Spider

Small agile hunters with excellent eyesight. They leap long distances to catch prey and rarely threaten humans.

Harmless

Tarantula

Large hairy spiders often kept as pets. Usually calm but may bite if threatened. Popular among enthusiasts.

Exotic / Pets

Wolf Spider

Fast hunting spiders that do not build webs. They rely on speed and strong eyesight to pursue prey.

Harmless

Garden Spider

Common in gardens and fields. They build beautiful orb webs and patiently wait for insects to land.

Harmless

Black Widow

Recognised by its shiny black body and red hourglass mark. Has strong venom but rarely bites unprovoked.

Venomous

Brown Recluse

Light brown with a violin-shaped marking. Its bite may cause significant tissue damage in severe cases.

Venomous

Sydney Funnel Web

Highly venomous spider from Australia found in moist sheltered spots like gardens and under logs.

Venomous

Brazilian Wandering Spider

A fast active hunter that does not build webs. Known for defensive behaviour and powerful venom.

Venomous
