Discover spider species, understand their behavior, learn safety tips, and explore the fascinating world of these amazing creatures.

Popular Spider Species

Jumping Spider

Small agile hunters with excellent eyesight. They leap long distances to catch prey.

Tarantula

Large hairy spiders often kept as pets. Usually calm but may bite if threatened.

Wolf Spider

Fast hunting spiders that do not build webs. They rely on speed and strong eyesight.

Garden Spider

Common in gardens and fields. They build beautiful webs and wait for insects.
