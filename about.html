What are spiders?

spiders are called arachnids,they have 8 legs and 2 body parts.Spiders help control insect by catching and eating them,often usind webs.

Where do spiders live?

Spiders can be found in various habitats around the world, including forests, grasslands, and even in human-made environments like homes and gardens.

dangers of spiders

While most spiders are harmless to humans, some species can be venomous and pose a threat to human health for example Black Widow Spider,brazilian wandering spider and brown recluse spider. spider bites may lead to allergic reactions or other serious health complications in some other people.

facts about spiders

Spiders are found on every continent except Antarctica. There are over 48,000 known species of spiders.

did you know?

Some spiders can live for several years, and the largest spider species can have a leg span of up to 12 inches (30 cm).

safety

When encountering spiders, it's important to remain calm and avoid sudden movements that might provoke them. If you have concerns about a spider in your home, consider contacting a pest control professional for assistance.
